Office Policies

FINANCIAL - It is your payment that allows us to continue providing high levels of professional care, maintain our facility, and to compensate our team. If for any reason you cannot keep your financial agreement, inform us immediately to avoid any misunderstanding. We will make every attempt to make affordable arrangements.

CARE PLAN - The doctor has designed a specific course of action to allow proper care. Missed appointments should be made up within 24 hours and MUST be made up within 7 days to protect your corrective rhythm. Cancellations of any treatment plans must be discussed directly with your doctor by either phone or in office. It is our policy, that we do not take cancellations of treatment plans over text.

FAMILY - We are absolutely committed to your family's health. The Well Adjusted Chiropractic Team recognizes that the children are the future. Because of this commitment, it is our highest priority that all individuals in our practice have their children checked in our office. This spinal and nerve system checkup will be done for $50 for any immediate family member within 14 business days of your commitment to care.

REFERRALS - The greatest honor a patient can give the doctor is the referral of their family or loved one. We promise to give them the same attention, care, and quality service that you receive. Thank you in advance!

CANCELLATIONS - We are committed to our mission to provide remarkable chiropractic care to everyone in our community. In order for this to happen, we ask that our patients give us at least a 24 hour notice for any cancellations. We understand life happens and we will do all we can to help reschedule in those situations. There will be a $50 cancellation fee if we do not receive a 24 hour notice.

MISSED APPOINTMENT POLICY - Adherence to the prescribed care plan, including the schedule of visits, is crucial for your treatment success. Your commitment is reflected in maintaining the recommended rhythm of adjustments, which is essential for positive outcomes. Should you need to reschedule an appt, we'll strive to rebook it for the same or next day. Missed appts must be compensated for within the same or following week to ensure you receive the recommended number of weekly adjustments. Failure to make up 3 missed appts will be taken as a sign of decreased commitment, leading to a pause of your care.

BETTER RESULTS FASTER WORKSHOP - As part of our commitment to enhancing your health and treatment outcomes, we require all new patients to attend our 'Better Results Faster' workshop. This informative session is designed to expedite your recovery by equipping you with essential knowledge on optimal health practices, chiropractic care benefits, and patient compliance. We offer these workshops weekly and will make every effort to accommodate your schedule, ensuring you can join at your earliest convenience.

Our team is dedicated to your health and success, and we count on your cooperation to achieve the best results. Thank you for your commitment to your health journey.
